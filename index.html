Project Overview

This project consisted on creating a dashboard that displayed information about bacteria that live inside the human's belly button from a sample of hundreds of people. As it can be seen below, the dashboard visualizes each person's demographic information, top 10 bacteria found, how frequently they wash their belly button, and the bacteria cultures per sample
